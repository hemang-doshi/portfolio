# Outseta Sunset-Marketplace Style Reference

## Direction

A warm golden-hour gradient opens the page, while Aubergine provides the
structural ink across text, borders, icons, and the final dark CTA. The interface
is product-led rather than image-led: white cards contain real UI compositions,
JetBrains Mono labels introduce sections, and a small number of Permanent Marker
annotations add a human studio-note layer inside the hero.

## Color Roles

- **Aubergine `#240029`** — primary text, icon strokes, borders, and dark CTA.
- **Fuchsia Signal `#df37a7`** — primary conversion buttons only.
- **Heather `#6d526d`** — secondary copy and interactive outlines.
- **Plum Tinted `#d4ccd4`** — default hairline and muted detail.
- **Canvas `#ffffff`** — page and card surface.
- **Glowstick `#ffcc11`** — terminal hero-gradient color only.
- **Forest/Mint/Honeydew** — genuine active, verified, or connected status only.

## Typography

- **Boldonse** fills the Kaio display role at responsive 36–80px sizes.
- **Inter** is the only body and interface family.
- **JetBrains Mono** labels sections at 12px with 0.10em tracking.
- **Permanent Marker** appears only in one to three hero annotations.
- **Phosphor Icons** use Aubergine and regular or bold weights as appropriate.

## Material Hierarchy

- Cards: 14px radius and purple-tinted shadows.
- Buttons: 6px radius.
- Inputs: 3px radius.
- Tags and status badges: 999px radius.
- Showcase preview images: 6px internal radius.

## Layout

- Full-width page bands with a centered 1200px content container.
- Comfortable 80px section rhythm and 16px card gaps.
- Three-column hero showcase, two-column work/evidence grids, and one-column mobile collapse.
- Only one dark band: the final Aubergine contact CTA.

## Do

- Use the exact 145-degree sunset gradient only for the hero.
- Keep Fuchsia to one primary action per viewport.
- Tint shadows and borders with the purple brand family.
- Use UI previews as evidence rather than decorative abstraction.
- Preserve visible focus states and readable content without motion.

## Do Not

- Do not introduce neutral black borders or shadows.
- Do not apply the sunset gradient to cards, buttons, badges, or icons.
- Do not place handwritten annotations outside the hero.
- Do not invent a second display family, extra material radius, or decorative accent palette.
- Do not fabricate metrics, testimonials, client names, project URLs, or article URLs.
